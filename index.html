Welcome

My name is Victoria Sanchez, and I am the owner of De Novo Beauty. I am a creative and professional makeup artist who strives to make everyone feel comfortable in their skin. Makeup can be intimidating to people who haven’t experimented with it, so I will always explain every step to ensure it’s what you want! I offer makeup for all events, including weddings, photo shoots, parties, and more! Anyone and everyone are welcome in my chair! I’m so excited to meet you and tailor your experience!

About Me

I am originally from San Diego, CA, but I have resided in Denver, CO for more than 15 years. My passion for makeup started at a young age, and through hard work and dedication, I turned that passion into a full-fledged career. I have received my Esthetician's certficate in October of 2021, graduating as the valedictorian with the highest volume of sales and services out of all five classes. After graduating, I received my license the following January and immediately started getting to work! I was a licensed makeup artist at Sephora for two years, perfecting my craft. I specialize in combining and customizing services for each client, giving each person a unique and personal experience that will leave them satisfied and confident in their looks. I strive to make everyone and everyone feel comfortable and welcome in my space.
